## 🎯 What is WPOS?

**WPOS** (Workforce Performance Operating System) is an **enterprise-grade SaaS platform** that transforms how organizations manage, diagnose, and optimize workforce performance. It replaces fragmented HR tools, spreadsheets, and annual review cycles with a **continuous, AI-powered diagnostic workflow** — from detecting a KPI gap to implementing a targeted intervention and measuring the outcome.

### The One-Line Pitch
> **"WPOS is the operating system for workforce performance — it tells you WHO is underperforming, WHY it's happening, and WHAT to do about it, before it's too late."**

## 🏗️ Core Workflow: The 7-Step Diagnostic Pipeline

This is the heart of WPOS — a structured methodology that ensures **no performance issue goes undiagnosed**.

### Step 1: DETECT — Automated Gap Detection
- KPIs are tracked per employee with target vs actual values
- System automatically flags **RED** (>10% below target), **YELLOW** (0-10% below), **GREEN** (on/above target)
- **Sparkline trends** detect 3-period declining patterns
- **Proactive Risk Prediction** calculates breach probability before the actual breach

### Step 2: EVIDENCE — Multi-Source Data Collection
- **6 evidence types**: Quantitative (CRM data), Qualitative (manager observations), Behavioral (attendance), System-generated (audit logs), Contextual (restructuring, tools), Temporal (seasonal patterns)
- Each evidence item scored for **reliability** (High/Medium/Low)
- **Evidence Impact Scoring**: 0-10 score based on keyword analysis across 6 themes
- **Correlation Heatmap**: Shows co-occurrence patterns between evidence types

### Step 3: DIAGNOSE — AI-Powered Root Cause Analysis
- **Guided 4-Step Wizard**: Select employee → Attach evidence → Choose categories → Generate hypotheses
- **10 Root Cause Categories**: Skill Gap, Knowledge Gap, Process Issue, Tool Issue, Environmental, Resource, Management, Motivation, Workload, Policy
- **Hypothesis Generation Engine**: Analyzes evidence keywords, scores each category, ranks top 5 by confidence
- **Confidence Score**: 0-100% based on evidence strength and alignment
- **Maturity Level**: 1-5 based on evidence completeness

### Step 4: REVIEW — Manager Approval
- Diagnostic report submitted for manager review
- Manager can **approve** (enables case creation) or **reject with reason** (returns to analyst)
- Full audit trail of who reviewed, when, and decision

### Step 5: CASE — Structured Case Management
- **Auto-created from approved diagnostic** with one click
- **Auto-priority**: Critical/High/Medium/Low based on confidence score
- **Root cause linked** from top hypothesis
- **Case lifecycle**: Open → Investigate → Action Planned → Monitoring → Resolved
- Sequential case numbers (CAS-2026-0001)

### Step 6: INTERVENE — Targeted Actions
- **Intervention Library**: Training, Coaching, Mentoring, Process Redesign, Tool Upgrade, Time Management
- **Action Plans**: Numbered items with owners, deadlines, progress tracking
- **Bilingual**: Arabic + English names for every intervention

### Step 7: FOLLOW-UP — Measure Impact
- **30/60/90-day check-ins** with before/after KPI comparison
- **Improvement percentage** calculated automatically
- **Close or escalate** based on results
- Full cycle complete — data feeds back into Step 1 for continuous improvement
